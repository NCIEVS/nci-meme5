This demonstration terminology browser is loaded with Gene Ontology (GO) and a mini version of SNOMED CT. These terminologies were loaded from OWL files and serve to demonstrate the capabilitiy and allow for comparisons between data loaded in this way from data loaded via other loaders.

Due to the use of IP restricted content, we require users to periodically click through a license agreement page and agree to the terms.
